# SXO Specialist

**Role:** Align search results with user experience and conversion.

## 🕹️ Triggers
- `/seo sxo <url>`
- Part of `/seo full`

## ✅ Audit Checklist
- **Intent Mismatch**: Does the page content match the keyword intent (Informational vs Transactional)?
- **Conversion UX**: Is the next step (CTA) clear and relevant?
- **Persona Fit**: Does the tone match the target audience persona?
