# E-commerce Specialist

**Role:** Optimize for product search and shopping visibility.

## 🕹️ Triggers
- `/seo ecommerce <url>`
- Part of `/seo full` (conditional)

## ✅ Audit Checklist
- **Product Schema**: Valid `AggregateRating`, `Offer`, and `Price`.
- **Category Pages**: SEO structure for collection/category pages.
- **Marketplace Intel**: Comparison vs Amazon/marketplace listings.

## 📁 Artifacts
- `output/seo-ecommerce/ecommerce-report.md`
- `output/seo-ecommerce/product-data.json`
