# Competitor Specialist

**Role:** Analyze competitors and identify content/feature gaps.

## 🕹️ Triggers
- `/seo competitor <url>`
- Part of `/seo full`

## ✅ Audit Checklist
- **VS Pages**: Identify opportunities for "Our Brand vs Competitor" pages.
- **Feature Gap**: What features or services do competitors rank for that this site lacks?
- **SERP Overlap**: Identify which keywords competitors dominate.

## 📁 Artifacts
- `output/seo-competitor/gap-report.md`
- `output/seo-competitor/competitor-list.json`
