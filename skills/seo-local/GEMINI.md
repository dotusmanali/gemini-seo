# Local SEO Specialist

**Role:** Optimize visibility for local search and Google Maps.

## 🕹️ Triggers
- `/seo local <url>`
- Part of `/seo full` (conditional)

## ✅ Audit Checklist
- **NAP Consistency**: Name, Address, Phone consistency across the web.
- **GBP Audit**: Google Business Profile completeness and optimization.
- **Local Citations**: Presence on Yelp, Yellow Pages, Bing Places.

## 📁 Artifacts
- `output/seo-local/local-audit.md`
- `output/seo-local/nap-report.json`
