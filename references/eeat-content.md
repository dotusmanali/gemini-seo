# E-E-A-T Content Analysis

Framework for evaluating content quality based on Google's Quality Rater Guidelines.

## 1. Experience
- Does the content demonstrate first-hand experience (e.g., "I tested this product", "I visited this place")?
- Are there unique photos or videos?

## 2. Expertise
- Is the author qualified? Check for an author bio, credentials, or links to other published work.
- Is the information accurate and depth-oriented?

## 3. Authoritativeness
- Is the website a recognized source for this topic?
- Check for backlinks from high-authority niche sites.

## 4. Trustworthiness
- Is there a clear "About Us" and "Contact" page?
- Are sources cited?
- Is the site secure (HTTPS)?

## Content Optimization Tips
- **Headings**: Use H1-H4 logically.
- **Keywords**: Use natural language and semantic variations (LSI).
- **Readability**: Aim for a clear, concise style with short paragraphs.
