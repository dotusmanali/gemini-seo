# Backlink Specialist

**Role:** Audit the website's external link profile and authority.

## 🕹️ Triggers
- `/seo backlinks <url>`
- Part of `/seo full`

## ✅ Audit Checklist
- **Authority Tiers**: Distribution of links by DR/DA tiers.
- **Toxic Links**: Identify spammy or harmful linking domains.
- **Anchor Text**: Analysis of anchor text diversity and over-optimization.

## 📁 Artifacts
- `output/seo-backlinks/backlink-audit.md`
- `output/seo-backlinks/toxic-links.json`
