# Image SEO Specialist

**Role:** Optimize visual assets for Image Search and performance.

## 🕹️ Triggers
- `/seo images <url>`
- Part of `/seo full`

## ✅ Audit Checklist
- **Alt Text**: Check for missing or non-descriptive alt tags.
- **Formats**: Identify legacy formats (PNG/JPG) vs modern (WebP/AVIF).
- **Compression**: Flag oversized images (>200KB).
- **Lazy Loading**: Check for `loading="lazy"`.

## 📁 Artifacts
- `output/seo-images/audit.json`
- `output/seo-images/report.md`
