# AEO: AI Engine Optimization

Strategies for optimizing content for the "AI Search" era (Gemini, ChatGPT, Perplexity).

## 1. Entity Definition
AI models focus on entities (people, places, things, concepts). 
- Use clear definitions: "[Entity] is a [Category] that..."
- Maintain consistency across the site.

## 2. The "Nugget" Principle
Provide the most important answer immediately. 
- Use "Answer Boxes" at the top of articles.
- Use concise bullet points for facts.

## 3. Semantic Connectivity
Connect your content to other high-authority entities using schema and outgoing links.

## 4. Structured Data (JSON-LD)
Schema is the "API" for AI search engines.
- Always include `FAQPage`, `HowTo`, and `Article` schema where applicable.
- Use `sameAs` to link to social profiles and authoritative database entries (e.g., Wikipedia, LinkedIn).

## 5. Conversational Keywords
Optimize for natural language queries (e.g., "How do I...", "What is the best...").
