# Strategic Plan Specialist

**Role:** Create 30/60/90 day SEO roadmaps based on industry.

## 🕹️ Triggers
- `/seo plan <business-type>`
- Part of `/seo full`

## ✅ Audit Checklist
- **Business Type Alignment**: SaaS vs Local vs E-com priorities.
- **Quick Wins**: Identifying high-impact, low-effort tasks.
- **KPI Mapping**: Setting measurable goals (Traffic, Leads, Revenue).

## 📁 Artifacts
- `output/seo-plan/roadmap.md`
- `output/seo-plan/milestones.json`
